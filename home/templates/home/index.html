About

"What should I have for dinner?" It is a common question we face every day. A simple question, however, sometimes we might find it's hard to solve. So, we designed a web app that helps people with their eating decisions. Furthermore the application not only helps the users make a choice, but also helps them understand the food they eat every day more and the relations between it.

We hope you can find joy while exploring the dishes in our application and become inspired. Find out how healthy the food you eat is and have "What do I eat today?" become a fun part of your day. No longer an annoyance!!
