Tutorial


Since you have come this far that means you have already selected a food! First off the food network is meant for you to explore, so feel free to click on everything and ignore this tutorial.

However you may still be left with questions. In that case continue in this tutorial.

There is three different entry points to enter the food network:

1. You take the questionnaire and end up on specific food that you already like.
2. You click the random button and enter at a random point.
3. You pick from a a list of available dishes in the list everything section.

No matter which option you chose you will end up on the network. Also it should be noted that at any point these options are available at the left hand side of the screen.

The currently selected dish is displayed in the middle, slightly larger than the other images with the arrows pointing outwards. Related dishes are ordered in a fan pattern around this dish. You can click these at any time to make them the currently selected dish and show suggestions. When hovering over the arrows you can check which relation these two dishes hold (to be implemented). On the right hand side more information about the currently selected dish is diplayed, such as the ingredients, a picture if available, the country of origin and a link to the source recipe!

We hope that with this information you can resume on your journey and find something of your liking!
